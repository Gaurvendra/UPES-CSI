Hackathon

UPES-CSI organizes the biggest hackathon in the state of Uttarakhand every year. The 48-hour event filled with coding, enthusiasm and teamwork where exceptional minds look for creative and innovative solutions for industrial problem statements.

Hackathon 1.0

Back in 2017, UPES-CSI initiated the first-ever hackathon organised in UPES, Dehradun. It was a new experience for all the students as they encountered such out of the box ideas. It was a 24-hour continuous coding festival where a lot of tech-enthusiasts participated and made this experimental event a huge success.











Hackathon 2.0

After the super success of our first edition of Hackathon, UPES-CSI was back again with Hackathon 2.0. This time, it was bigger and better than the last year. A lot of industrial specialists came and guided our participants. It was the game of creativity mixed with technical skills. It was difficult for our panellists to decide the result as the competition was so tough. Everybody was at their very best.











Hackathon 3.0

Organising Hackathon was now a trademark for UPES-CSI. With growing tech-enthusiasts and the demand for more such enthralling events, UPES-CSI was back again with Hackathon 3.0. This time, UPES was experiencing a different wave altogether regarding these type of brain-storming events. From amazing participation to our college faculties acting as the mentors to our students, it was hugely successful.











Hackathon 4.0

This year, the team of UPES-CSI wanted it to be the biggest ever hackathon. With out-of-the-box problem statements to the arrival of big tech companies like Xebia and Postman, Hackathon 4.0 was at a different level altogether. We experienced the best ever ideas from our participants in this 48-hour coding festival. Not only that, but students grabbed some internship opportunities as well.

Clickathon

You don't take a photograph, you make it" - Ansel Adams Camera is your mirror with a memory. An event, solely for “Photophiles”, Clickathon is one of those events where some amazing photography-enthusiasts capture their best shots.

Clickathon 1.0

Inaugurated in 2019, this was a great event for all the photography enthusiasts out there. It tested your patience and nerves to your skills and experience. You have to be focussed as a leopard to get the perfect click. In the end, it all depends on the picture presentation and the theme justification.











Clickathon 2.0

After the success of Clickathon 1.0, UPES-CSI organised its second edition with double enthusiasm and avidity. It experienced huge participation not only from UPES, but from around the State. With the “Lockdown” theme, participants in this 2-day event were directed to click a portrait depicting a small story.
